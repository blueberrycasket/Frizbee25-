About FrizBee'25

Frizbee'25, the annual national level flagship event of IEEE Student Branch of MES College of Engineering Kuttippuram. Frizbee'25 is a celebration of innovation and creativity, bringing together the brightest minds from across the country to showcase their technical and non-technical skills. With a plethora of events, competitions and workshops, this is a one-of-a-kind platform that provides a glimpse into the future of technology. This year, Frizbee'25 promises to be bigger and better than ever before, with a lineup of exciting events.

Workshops

Robotics Workshop

The Robotics Workshop is a hands-on introduction to the fascinating world of robotics, guiding participants through the basics of designing, building, and programming simple robots. Attendees will explore essential concepts such as sensors, actuators, electronic components, and basic coding, all through engaging practical activities. By the end of the workshop, participants will have a solid foundation in robotics and an understanding of how these technologies are shaping the future.

Embedded Systems Workshop

The Embedded Systems Workshop provides a hands-on introduction to the fundamentals of embedded technology, focusing on designing and programming smart electronic devices. Participants will discover how embedded systems power everyday gadgets, explore essential concepts like microcontrollers, sensors, and basic coding, and learn how to interface hardware and software for functional projects. By the end of the workshop, attendees will understand the potential of embedded systems in applications ranging from smart homes to industry, gaining foundational skills for building intelligent, automated solutions in the modern world.
